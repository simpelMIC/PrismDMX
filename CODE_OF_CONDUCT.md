# PrismDMX Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of the PrismDMX project, we pledge to foster an inclusive and welcoming community. We value the participation of everyone and strive to provide a positive experience for all contributors and users.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants in the PrismDMX community to adhere to the following standards:

1. **Respectful Behavior:** Treat all individuals with respect and kindness, regardless of background, identity, or beliefs.

2. **Inclusive Environment:** Embrace diversity and promote inclusivity within our community. We welcome contributions from people of all backgrounds.

3. **Open Communication:** Encourage open and constructive communication. Be considerate of others' viewpoints and engage in discussions with empathy and understanding.

4. **Constructive Feedback:** Provide feedback in a constructive manner. Offer suggestions and critiques that contribute to the improvement of the project and respect the efforts of others.

5. **Professional Conduct:** Conduct yourself professionally and refrain from engaging in any form of harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable within the PrismDMX community:

1. Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other personal characteristic.

2. Use of derogatory language, insults, or personal attacks.

3. Disruptive behavior that impedes the progress of the project or creates a hostile environment for others.

4. Any form of spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at [contact email or other contact information]. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly. The project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject any contributions or comments that violate this code of conduct.

Participants who engage in repeated or severe violations of the code of conduct may be temporarily or permanently excluded from participation in the PrismDMX community.

## Conclusion

We believe that by upholding these standards and fostering a culture of respect and collaboration, we can create a vibrant and inclusive community around PrismDMX. Thank you for your contributions and commitment to making PrismDMX a welcoming environment for everyone.
